feat: rate-limit login attempts (6 / 15 min)

Login had no throttling, so passwords could be brute-forced. Added an
in-memory sliding-window limiter (same mechanism as the password-reset
limiter): 6 attempts per 15 minutes, keyed by both email and IP. Over
the limit returns 429 Too Many Requests; a successful login clears the
email counter. The controller forwards req.ip exactly like
forgot-password does.

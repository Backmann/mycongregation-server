feat: let any publisher manage their own absence

Absences drive scheduling: anyone building assignment/duty/cleaning
schedules needs to see who is away. Previously only managers
(body_coordinator / life_ministry_overseer / secretary, plus admin)
could create or edit absences, so ordinary publishers couldn't record
their own time away.

Authorization moves from a blanket responsibility guard on the
controller into the service: managers may write any publisher's absence;
everyone else may write only their own (the publisher linked to their
user). Ownership for update/delete/restore is checked against the STORED
publisher_id, not the request body, and a non-manager cannot reassign an
absence to another publisher. Reading stays open so absence warnings keep
surfacing in every editor. Adds a service spec covering the matrix
(own allowed, others forbidden, manager/admin allowed).
